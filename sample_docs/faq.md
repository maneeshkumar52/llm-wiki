# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I request access to a shared dashboard?

Submit a request in the internal IT portal and include the dashboard name, business reason, and your manager's approval.

## Where are onboarding guides stored?

All onboarding guides are stored in the Knowledge Enablement workspace under the folder named `New Joiner Pack`.

## How often are usage metrics refreshed?

Standard usage metrics are refreshed every four hours. Executive summary dashboards are refreshed once every morning at 7:00 AM.

## Who owns the platform roadmap?

The product operations council reviews roadmap priorities each month, while the platform director signs off on quarterly roadmap changes.
