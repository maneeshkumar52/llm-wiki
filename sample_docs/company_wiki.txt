Acme Analytics Company Wiki

Mission
Acme Analytics helps operations teams reduce repetitive reporting by turning raw internal data into dashboards, summaries, and weekly decision briefs.

Support Hours
The internal support desk operates Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM India Standard Time.

Incident Policy
Critical incidents must be acknowledged within 15 minutes and escalated to the platform lead if not mitigated within 60 minutes.

Security Guidelines
Employees must use the company password manager and enable multi-factor authentication on every approved SaaS tool.

Release Process
Product releases happen every Thursday. Emergency hotfixes require approval from one engineering manager and one product manager.
