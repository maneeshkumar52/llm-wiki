# Sample Questions

Use these questions to validate the app quickly after loading the bundled sample documents.

## Quick smoke questions

- What are the internal support hours?
- How often are usage metrics refreshed?
- Who owns the platform roadmap?
- What security guidelines should employees follow?
- Explain retrieval augmented generation in simple terms.

## Questions that test cross-document reasoning

- Compare the company support policy with the FAQ refresh cadence.
- Which sample documents are most relevant for onboarding a new support engineer?
- What operational details are defined in the company wiki versus the FAQ?
- Summarize the main ideas about local AI knowledge systems from the sample docs.

## Questions for a real team handbook workspace

- What are our incident response SLAs?
- Which dashboards refresh daily versus every few hours?
- What is the release approval process for emergency fixes?
- What tools require multi-factor authentication?
- Which document should a new employee read first for onboarding?

## Questions for a personal second-brain workspace

- What themes appear across my recent notes?
- Which notes mention the same concept under different names?
- What unanswered questions keep showing up in my research?
- Which saved answers should be promoted into durable wiki notes?

## How to test automated learning

1. Ask a question that returns a good answer.
2. Click `Save Last Answer to Wiki` in the sidebar.
3. Open the `Wiki` tab and confirm a new page appears under `Learned Notes`.
4. Ask a follow-up question that can benefit from the newly saved note.
5. Confirm the workspace now has one more learned note in the header metrics.
